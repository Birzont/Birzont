Birzont Implementation Guide (Quick)

1) Suggested Terms Consent Copy (English)
- "By creating an account or continuing, you agree to the Birzont Terms of Service and acknowledge the Privacy Policy."
- "By purchasing, you authorize recurring charges (if applicable), agree that all sales are final except where required by law, and consent to Birzont's refund terms."

2) EU/UK Withdrawal Waiver Copy (English)
- "If you are in the EU/UK (or another jurisdiction with a statutory cooling-off right), you may have a 14-day withdrawal right. By requesting immediate access and using paid features/credits during that period, you expressly request early performance and acknowledge that your withdrawal right is waived to the extent permitted by law."

3) Recommended DB Fields for Legal Evidence
- user_id
- email
- region_country_code
- is_eu_or_uk_user (boolean)
- terms_version
- privacy_version
- refund_policy_version
- agreed_at (timestamp, UTC)
- agreed_ip
- agreed_user_agent
- agreed_locale
- purchase_id / subscription_id
- waiver_shown (boolean)
- waiver_accepted (boolean)
- waiver_accepted_at (timestamp, UTC)
- first_paid_use_at (timestamp, UTC)
- first_credit_use_at (timestamp, UTC)
- consent_event_hash (immutable audit hash)

Operational note:
- Store consent logs as append-only records (do not overwrite), and keep historical versions of legal documents that each user agreed to.
